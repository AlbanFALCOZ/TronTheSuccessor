how the ranking system works

victories

Win 1v1 matches to earn Tron Points (TP). You'll earn TP per win based on your performance.

defeats

Losing matches will cost you TP depending on the skill difference between you and your opponent.

rank progression

As you accumulate TP, you'll naturally progress through the tiers and ranks.

Tron Points (TP)

Tron Points are the measure of your progress in the ranked system. You need 100 TP to advance to the next tier or rank. TP is earned through victories and lost through defeats.

Each division (except Hexagon) has three tiers: III, II, and I. You'll start at tier III of each division and work your way up to tier I before advancing to the next division.
